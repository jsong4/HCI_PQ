How Polar Pals Works

Connecting with others isn't always easy, but we're here to make it simpler—and more fun.

Polar Pals is built on three guiding pillars: transparency in our matchmaking process, algorithmic accuracy to ensure viable connections, and a super customizable experience so every user can use Polar Pals in a way that feels just right for them.

  1. Create your anonymous Polar Pal Persona by doing our personality and preferences survey.
  2. Explore your *perfect* matches, automatically processed and generated by our super-fast systems.
  3. Explore prompts , customize your Polar Bear Profile, and jump into chats — all designed to help you get to know your matches on your own terms!
